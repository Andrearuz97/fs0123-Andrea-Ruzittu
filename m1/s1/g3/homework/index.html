Focaccia with caramelized onions,
cranberries, and brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-porpouse flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil plus mor fore the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (srape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
